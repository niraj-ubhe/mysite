MOST WATCHED ANIME

Kimetsu No Yaiba

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is story about a boy named as Tanjro Komado, whose family is devoured by a demon. Only his younger sister, Nezuko survies but she is no longer a human. She had transformed into a demon too. But Nezuko is special she is a demon which will never devour humans. And from that day on Tanjiro decides to be a Demon slayer and find a way to transform Nezeko back into a human
